view_kanban KanbanIT!

This is just a simple Kanban related application (similar to Trello but very bare bones).

You can use the Kanban features after you sign in (you can sign in with Google).
This was a super fun project! I learned a bunch out of both the Angular framework and the Google Firebase BaaS.
There are certainly some small things that could be added and improved upon (404 redirection, better UI in certain pages, better color palette, etc.) but I have bunch of other projects that I need to attend to, which is why I can't keep coding and designing this to perfection.
